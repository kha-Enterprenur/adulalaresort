The Adulala Philosophy

A beautiful sanctuary where modern comfort meets raw nature.

Located along the beautiful volcanic lakes of Bishoftu, Adulala Resort & Spa is a perfect mountain hideaway. Enjoy quiet spaces, warm hospitality, and healthy fresh air.

We design our spaces with natural basalt stone, rich local wood, and bright gold accents that glow beautifully under the Ethiopian sun.
